How to make a Text Adventure (using HTML)

The first step of making a text adventure, well, of this one at least, is to think of some characters that would be fun to write about, what they might be like, and how much player choice affects that character and the story in general. As an example, I started out by choosing some Pokemon that I like and know a lot about.
